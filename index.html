About Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi, born on June 24, 1987, is an Argentine professional football football (soccer) player widely regarded as one of the greatest players in the history of the sport. He has spent themajority of his career playing as a forward for FC Barcelona and has recently joined Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) in 2021.

Work

  • 10-time La Liga champion with FC Barcelona
  • 4-time UEFA Champions League winner
  • 6-time Ballon d'Or recipient

Resume

  • 2004-2021: FC Barcelona
  • 2021-present: Paris Saint-Germain (PSG)
